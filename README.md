# Hi, I'm Angelo

Systems Engineering student at **UAI** (Buenos Aires) and AI Agent Developer at **Botmaker**. I work at the intersection of conversational AI, education, and robotics — building LLM-powered agents by day, teaching Algebra and Calculus as a lab assistant, and prototyping vision and robotics systems at UAI's CAETI lab.

I care about shipping things that solve real problems: educational chatbots that actually help students pass, chess-playing robots that connect computer vision to physical hardware, and automation pipelines that save real hours.
